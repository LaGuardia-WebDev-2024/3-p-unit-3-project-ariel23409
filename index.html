Recipe #1: Coconut macaroons with chocolate chips

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 20-22
Ingredients Quantity
  • Sweetened coconut
  • Vanilla extract
  • Condensed sweetened milk
  • Chocolate chips
  • Melting chocolate (optional)
  • Flaked coconut
  • All purpose flour
  • 1 14oz bag of sweetened coconut
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 14 oz can of condensed sweetened milk
  • 1-2 cups of chocolate chips

Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
Step 2: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line cookie sheets with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
Step 3: Stir together coconut, flour, and salt in a large bowl. Mix in sweetened condensed milk and vanilla using your hands until well blended.
Step 4: Use a small ice cream scoop to drop coconut mixture onto the prepared cookie sheets.
Step: 5: Bake in the preheated oven until coconut is toasted, about 12 to 15 minutes .
Step 6: Eat it up!

Recipe #2: Strawberry chessecake cookies

  • Time: 15
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
    Cookies
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Unsalted butter
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract
  • Strawberries

    • Cream chesse filling
  • Cream chesse
  • Unsalted butter
  • White sugar
  • Zest of one lemon
  • lemon juice
    Cookies
  • 1 1/2 cup of flour
  • 1/2 tsp of baking Powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup brown and white sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 strawberries, roughly chopped

    • Cream chesse filling
  • 7.05 oz of cream chesse
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup of white sugar
  • Zest of one lemon
  • 1 tbsp of lemon juice

Cookies
Step 1: Preheat the oven to 180C / 355F
Step 2: Combine butter, white sugar, and brown sugar in the bowl of a stand mixer, beat on high speed until light and fluffy
Step 3: Add egg and vanilla, beat until combined
Step 4: Add flour, baking powder and baking soda, mix until just combined
Step 5: Gently fold in chopped strawberries
Step 6: Cover bowl with cling wrap and chill for at least 1 hour
Step 7: Remove and shape into 12 balls, flatten slightly
Step 8: Place on a baking tray lined with baking paper (make sure to leave enough space between each ball for the cookie to spread)
Step 9: Bake for 12-13 minutes until golden brown
Step 10: Cool completely
Cream chesse filling
Step 1: Combine the cream cheese, butter, sugar, lemon juice and zest in the bowl of a stand mixer
Step 2: Beat on medium speed for 3-4 minutes until smooth and creamy
Step 3: Transfer to a piping bag fitted with a large round tip
Step 4: Pipe a large dollop on the bottom of a cookie and top with a matching sized cookie
Step 5: Place in an airtight container and store in the fridge until it's time to eat!

Recipe #3: Caramel apple cream puffs

  • Time: 1 hr and 30 mins
  • Serves: 14 (approx.)
Ingredients Quantity
    Pate a choux puffs
  • Water
  • Unsalted butter
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • All purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
    • Cream filling
  • 3 egg yolks
  • Granulated sugar
  • Cornstarch
  • Kosher salt
  • Milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Heavy whipping cream
    • Toppings
  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of kosher salt
  • Salted caramel sauce
    Pate a choux puffs
  • 1 cup of Water
  • 1/2 cup of Unsalted butter
  • 1/2 tsp of Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp of Salt
  • 1 cup of all purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
    • Cream filling
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup of Granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp of Cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp Kosher salt
  • 3/4 cups of Milk
  • 1/2 tsp of Vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of Heavy whipping cream
    • Toppings
  • 1 tbsp of Unsalted butter
  • 3 1/2 apples
  • 2 tbsp of Granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp Ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of kosher salt
  • 4 oz of salted caramel sauce

Pate e choux puffs
Step 1: Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F, and line 2 baking sheets with parchment.
Step 2: Place the water, butter, sugar, and salt in a large saucepan.
Step 3: Cook over medium-high heat, until the butter is completely melted and the mixture is simmering.
Step 4: Add in the flour all at once and continue to cook, stirring, for about 5 minutes, or until the dough gathers into a ball and a film begins to form on the bottom of the pot.
Step 5: Transfer the mixture to a mixing bowl, and beat on medium speed.
Step 6: Drop in the eggs, one at a time. Allow each egg to become fully incorporated before adding the next (about 30 seconds to a minute).
Step 7: Pipe or spoon the dough onto parchment-lined baking sheets, allowing about 3 inches in between each puff.
Step 8: Bake for 25-35 minutes, or until very dry looking, puffed, and deep golden brown.
Cream filling
Step 1: Place the yolks, sugar, cornstarch, and salt in a medium bowl and whisk to combine.
Step 2: Heat the milk in a small pot until small bubbles form around the edge, and wisps of steam are rising from the surface.
Step 3: Add the hot milk to the egg mixture, a little at a time, whisking to combine.
Step 4: When all the milk has been added, transfer the mixture back to the pot and place over medium-low heat.
Step 5: Cook the pastry cream, whisking, until thickened.
Step 6: Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla.
Step 7: Pour the pastry cream through a fine mesh strainer, into a heat-safe bowl. Use the back of a ladle to press the mixture through the sieve.
Step 8: Press a layer of plastic wrap directly onto the surface, and refrigerate until completely cool (about 2 hours)
Step 9: Whip the cream until it holds stiff peaks.
Step 10: Fold the cooled pastry cream into the whipped cream.
Toppings
Step 1: Melt the butter in a large skillet, over medium heat.
Step 2: Add the apples, sugar, cinnamon, and salt.
Step 3: Cook, stirring occasionally, until the apples have softened slightly (about 5 to 10 minutes).
Step 4: Slice the pate a choux puffs in half horizontally and layer the bottom half with 5 to 8 slices of cooked apples and about 1/4 cup of cream filling.
Step 5: Sandwich with the top half of the puff and drizzle with Simply Perfect Salted Caramel Sauce.
